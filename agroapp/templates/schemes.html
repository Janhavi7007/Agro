Soil Health Card Scheme

Helps farmers understand the fertility status of their soil and recommends appropriate nutrients to improve productivity sustainably.

Apply

How to Apply: Submit a soil sample at the nearest Kisan Center or register through the Soil Health Card portal.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Apply

How to Apply: You can apply for the PMFBY scheme online through the official portal [https://pmfby.gov.in](https://pmfby.gov.in) or at your nearest CSC/agriculture department office.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Offers short-term credit to farmers to meet their agriculture and allied needs with flexible repayment options and low interest.

Apply

How to Apply:Apply online via your bank's KCC portal or visit the nearest branch with Aadhaar, land records, and a passport-size photo.
